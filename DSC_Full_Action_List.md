## Notes on Using This List

- **Critical** priority items are the ones that block other work or represent the core DSC value proposition (24hr turnaround, hiring, email warm-up) — these should never slip without immediate escalation.
- Several June actions are marked **"pending hire"** with Davis as interim owner. As each role is filled, reassign these directly to the new hire rather than leaving them with Davis — this is intentional, not a placeholder to ignore.
- This list assumes the three open roles (AI & Automation Lead, Outreach/RevOps Lead, Content Manager) are filled by mid-to-late June. If hiring slips, several July actions will need their due dates pushed accordingly — worth flagging in your weekly check-in rather than letting dates quietly slide.
- Action items are intentionally more granular than the original 19-action high-level workplan — this is meant to be the operational layer underneath that strategic view, not a replacement for it.
